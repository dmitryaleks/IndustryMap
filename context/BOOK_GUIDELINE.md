# Book guideline

This book explains how the supply chain for the semiconductor chip manufacturing process works and maps key Japanese companies to that process, explaining both technical aspects as well as the economy of the supply chain in semiconductors industry.

## Target audience

Investor and technology enthusiasts aiming to know the fundamentals of the semiconductor industry, not afraid of getting into technical details.

## Target companies to be included throughout the book

Companies listen in the data/database.json and CMP-*.json files under data/companies.

## What is covered for each company

  - The brief history of the company
  - What are the key focus areas and technologies and products
  - Who are the suppliers, vendors, clients, competitors

## The style of the book

Educational, with focus on technology, economics and finance of the industry and companies covered.
